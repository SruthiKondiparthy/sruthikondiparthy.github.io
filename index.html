Full Story ⌄
Problem Context
Expats in Germany face fragmented information, complex bureaucracy, and high cognitive load. User research (5+ interviews) revealed confusion around official processes, language barriers, and lack of structured guidance.
User Insights
• Bureaucracy confusion due to unclear forms and long processes
• Housing challenges including unfamiliar rental systems
• Language barriers in official communication
• Cultural adaptation and lack of support networks

Key Insight: Users consistently requested step-by-step guidance, reminders, and simplified language tools.
Market Opportunity
TAM: ~10M expats in Germany
SAM: ~3M English-speaking newcomers
SOM: ~500K initial tech-savvy users

Revenue: Freemium + premium subscriptions + relocation partnerships
Solution
AI-powered platform providing structured guidance across relocation workflows: • Step-by-step bureaucracy checklists
• Deadline tracking & reminders
• Letter scanner & translator (user-suggested feature)
• AI assistant for real-time help
• Multilingual support
Product Strategy
• User-centric development with continuous feedback loops
• Agile iterations with 2-week sprints
• Data-driven prioritization

Roadmap: MVP → Personalization → Advanced features → AI career assistant

About Me

Hi, I'm Sruthi

Software Engineer · Product Manager · Frankfurt am Main

I grew up in India and earned my M.Tech in Computer Science. I spent 7 years as a backend developer, building telecom billing systems at Convergys, working on Motorola’s predictive text engine (iTap), and later developing Python integration pipelines at Studio Hamburg MCI in Germany.

I then moved to Germany, raised a child, and built a life in a new country and language. That experience gave me first-hand insight into the complex, fragmented problems I now solve through products — especially around workflows, clarity, and decision-making.

I returned with a clear focus: completed a Product Management Bootcamp (Digitale Leute, 2025), re-skilled in full-stack development, and built two AI products from zero — owning the full lifecycle from user research and product decisions to backend development and delivery. I bring engineering depth with product thinking, and take end-to-end ownership from problem to shipped solution.
